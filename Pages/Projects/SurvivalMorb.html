Description

"Survival morb" is a Unity3D game where you have to protect the electric generator of your base against wave of ennemies called "Morbs".
You can use a pistol with limited ammo which can be dropped by Morbs (They can also drop money).
This game can be played with friends, you can insert a username make a room and joining room from other players.

How it works ?

I used Photon Pun2 for the rooms system and the synchronization between players.
I personnaly made the "Wave script" wich is used to spawn Morbs when they all die, then increase their number with a customizable scale.
The game also use an isometric camera.
